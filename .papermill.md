## Composable Observation Functors

- `observe_right_censor()` — right-censoring at fixed or random time
- `observe_left_censor()` — left-censoring
- `observe_periodic()` — periodic inspection
- `observe_mixture()` — mix observation schemes with weights

These functors compose: practitioners configure observation protocols at analysis time. The mathematical justification for this composability is the subject of companion paper 03-observation-composition.
